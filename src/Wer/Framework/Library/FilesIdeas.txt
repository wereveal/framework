Files needs to be able to

1. return the path to a file
2. return the directory path inside the web root
3. return the contents of the file

Files can exist in the follow paths

$doc_root = $_SERVER['DOCUMENT_ROOT'];

$doc_root . $directory_path_specified . $file_name
$doc_root . /assets/themes/ . $theme_name . $directory_path_specified . $file_name
$doc_root . /assets/themes/ . $theme_name . $file_dir_name . $file_name
$doc_root . /../app/config/ . $file_name
$doc_root . /../app/namespaces/ . $namespace . $file_name
$doc_root . /../app/namespaces/ . $namespace . /Controllers/ . $file_name
$doc_root . /../app/namespaces/ . $namespace . /Views/ . $file_name
$doc_root . /../app/namespaces/ . $namespace . /Model/ . $file_name
$doc_root . /../app/namespaces/ . $namespace . /Library/ . $file_name
